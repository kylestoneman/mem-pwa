    Memcoder

    Memcoder is a Major System encoder by Kyle Stoneman. It is powered by a phonetic database created by Carnegie Mellon University.

    Be aware: Regional pronunciations may not match your pronunciation. Please confirm words match your desired phonetics.

    Memcoder works on the Moon!

    Download as an app to your mobile phone for full functionality without an internet connection. Select "Add to Home Screen" on iPhones, or accept the prompt on Androids.
